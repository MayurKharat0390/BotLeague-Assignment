CATEGORIES

M

MINI FIGHTERS

Featherweight combat robots up to 5kg. Built for speed, agility, and tactical maneuvers. Ideal for university students and intermediate builders.

JUNIOR INNOVATORS

Designed for secondary and high school students. Projects focusing on smart automation, ecological solutions, and helpful domestic gadgets.

YOUNG ENGINEERS

College and university division featuring advanced heavy combat machines (up to 30kg) and industrial grade autonomous challenges.

ROBO MINDS

AI-focused challenge. Robots must autonomously navigate dynamic mazes, identify targets, and make real-time decisions using machine vision.

SPORTS

COMPETITION DISCIPLINES

Robo Race

Wheeled autonomous robots speed through tracks filled with obstacles, ramps, and sharp turns. Fastest lap wins.

Line Follower

High-precision robots navigate intricate paths marked by black lines. Measures code efficiency and sensor accuracy.

RC Racing

Manually driven remote control buggies competing in head-to-head racing on dynamic off-road dirt arenas.

FPV Drone Racing

Pilots navigate micro-quadcopters through three-dimensional glowing obstacle courses at breakneck speeds.

Robo Hockey

Teams of two wheeled robots face off on a custom miniature pitch, shooting pucks to score goals in a thrilling match.

Robo War

The main event. Custom metallic beasts clash in an enclosed steel arena, using hammers, lifters, and spinners to destroy opponents.
